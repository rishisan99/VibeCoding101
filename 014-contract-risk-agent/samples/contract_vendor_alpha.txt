SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is between Alpha Analytics Pvt Ltd and Bright Stores Ltd.
The service provider will deliver monthly sales reports and dashboard support.
The agreement starts on June 1, 2026 and continues for 12 months.

Payment:
Bright Stores Ltd will pay INR 50,000 per month within 15 days of invoice.

Confidentiality:
Both parties must keep shared business data confidential.

Termination:
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

Liability:
The service provider's total liability will not exceed the fees paid in the last 3 months.

Dispute Resolution:
Any dispute will be discussed in good faith before legal action.
